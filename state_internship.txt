The International Internship Network (IIN) is an organization dedicated to the fields of international experiential education and work integrated learning . The IIN is a collaborative community of program providers, universities &amp; colleges, employers, governments and students which promotes best practices, professional development, diversity &amp; inclusion, and partnership in the field of International Internships. IIN also runs the International Internship Conference , an annual conference where leaders in the industry of Study &amp; Internship Abroad, students and host companies gather to network, shares ideas and best practice. The International Internship Network (IIN) has been designated a Recognised Organisation (RO) by Immigration, Refugees and Citizenship Canada (IRCC) to help promote the International Experience Canada (IEC) program. As an RO, IIN can help foreign youth apply for Working Holiday and Co-op/Internship permits to Canada, and support Canadian youth with the same permits abroad.
